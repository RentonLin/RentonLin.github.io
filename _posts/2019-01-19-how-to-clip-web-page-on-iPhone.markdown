* How to clip web on iPhone?
	* On Chrome
		* Install PDF Viewer
		* Tap share button on top right
		* Tap "Import to PDF Viewer"
		* Save to open pdf viewer
	* On Safari
		* Tap share button on bottom bar
		* Tap "Import to PDF Viewer"
			* Save and open pd viewer
		* Or tap "Create PDF"
	* How to enum an enum?

		* Then you can call allCases on enum type
